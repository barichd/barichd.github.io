Barich Assistive Technology

Daniel Barich (Kenyon '05) of Braille Assistive Technology has two years of experience in braille transcription and digitizing complex documents. While his primary specialty is braille music transcription, he also transcribes literary material and other formats as needed. After graduating from Kenyon with a music major he obtained an M. S. in Applied Statistics from the Rochester Institute of Technology. Now he is about to complete a Library of Congress certification in literary braille transcription. For free consultation and a price quote for a transcription project, please email Daniel at barichd@kenyon.edu .
